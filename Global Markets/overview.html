Global Markets

Full Service Brokerage

Britannia has been offering dealing services in derivatives since 1986. Located in the City of London. Authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority. A member of the Dubai Gold & Commodity Exchange (DGCX), London Stock Exchange and FIA Europe (Specialist desks covering each of these products include Agricultural and Soft Commodities, Energy, Securities, Foreign Exchange and Bullion).

Trading

Futures and Options

Britannia offers direct market access (DMA) to the most liquid futures and options exchanges in the world, including CME, ICE, Eurex, HKEX, SGX, DGCX, MEFF and IDEM.
