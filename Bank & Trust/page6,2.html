Banking Services

Banking Services

Britannia Bank & Trust, currently offers banking and fiduciary services to meet the needs of a diversified range of international clientele with a customized approach, in particular:

Traditional banking services
Online banking platform
Custody and Execution Services (Equities, bonds, mutual funds, hedge funds, and structured products)
Money Market (Current accounts, fixed term deposits and fiduciary deposits)
Foreign Exchange (Spot, Forward, and Swap)
OTC and derivatives instruments
Precious Metal trading & custody
Payments and transfers
Lombard loans
Derivatives and others

In addition to the acquisition and management of direct clients, the primary development target is represented by institutional clients: asset managers, funds, brokers and dealers.

A supplementary line of business is dedicated to reliable custody and execution services, along with other banking services for institutional clients such as funds, insurance companies and broker/dealers.

Britannia Bank & Trust holds an unrestricted Investment Fund Administrator (IFA) license to facilitate and/or arrange for the establishment of any of the Professional, Standard and SMART funds in The Bahamas and provide the necessary fund administration and custody services.
